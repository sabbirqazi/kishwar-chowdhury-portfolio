About Me

I am a 38-year-old participant of the cooking reality show MasterChef Australia I was born to Laila and Kamrul Chowdhury, who too were enthusiasts of cooking. They are also the founders of the Bangladeshi community in Victoria, Australia. My father is from Bangladesh, and was a freedom fighter. He moved to Australia about 50 years ago as a student and met my mother, who is from Kolkata. I was born and brought up here in Australia.
